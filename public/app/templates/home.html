Trauma Healing in Nepal

Acupuncturists who are interested in volunteering need to meet specific criteria. Volunteers will treat people in a community acupuncture setting in conditions that can be physically and mentally demanding and unusual for those accustomed to a traditional clinical setting. For those interested in the Military Stress Recovery (formerly Veterans) Project please email veterans@acuwithoutborders.org with your name and contact information, and whether you are interested in starting a clinic or joining an existing clinic as a volunteer.
