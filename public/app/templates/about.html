About Us

The aim of OnPoint is to promote awareness of service organizations who provide acupuncture to underserved populations around the world.

Misson Statement

Our mission is to encourage practitioners to volunteer and other site visitors to donate funds to support theses organizations' efforts. We believe acupuncture is a low cost, highly effective medicine that has the capacity to impact a wide range of illnesses.
